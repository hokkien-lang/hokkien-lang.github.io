Tones


Tones are marked by how they are pronounced in real-life speech (including syllables in post-sandhi tone).

Tone sandhi


In Hokkien, syllables undergo tone changes depending on their position within a sentence. Syllables at the end of a sentence typically retain their original tone, while other syllables often undergo tone sandhi.
